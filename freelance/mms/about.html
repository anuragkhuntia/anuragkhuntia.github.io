Who We Are

Founded in 2008, MMS Enterprises is a Government Approved Contractor & Supplier delivering excellence in construction, fabrication, electrical & mechanical works, carpentry, supply, and demolition projects.

With over 15 years of experience, we are trusted by government and private clients alike for our quality, safety standards, and timely project delivery. From large-scale infrastructure to specialized fabrication jobs, we bring precision, expertise, and reliability to every project.
